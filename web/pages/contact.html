Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

How long does delivery take?

Most deliveries arrive within 30 minutes. We guarantee delivery within 30 minutes or your order is free!

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash on delivery, credit/debit cards, and online payment methods including PayPal and digital wallets.

Can I track my order?

Yes! Once your order is confirmed, you can track it in real-time from our Orders page.

What's your refund policy?

If you're not satisfied with your order, contact us within 24 hours and we'll make it right with a refund or replacement.

Do you offer group orders?

Absolutely! You can order multiple items and we'll coordinate delivery for larger groups.

Are there any dietary options?

Yes, we offer vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and many other dietary options. Filter by preference on our menu page.
